Challenge 10: BlackJack: Play the game

Finish the game of BlackJack that we started in challenge 9.

  1. Loop and prompt the user if they want to hit (take another card) or stay (stop)
  2. Display their hand each time
  3. Deal cards for the dealer until they reach 17 or higher
  4. Determine and display who 'won'
